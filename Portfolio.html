Hello!
My journey as a photographer has been a lifelong quest to capture the extraordinary in the ordinary, to freeze fleeting moments in time, and to share the world's beauty as I see it. Based in the vibrant city of Hyderabad, India, I have a passion for travel and exploration, always seeking new perspectives and experiences to capture through my lens. I love filming myself as I journey through the world, creating a visual diary of my adventures. Every photograph I take is a precious memory, a piece of the past that shapes my ongoing story.
